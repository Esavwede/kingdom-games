GAME MODES

WORD SCRAMBLE

Unscramble biblical words, names, and phrases. Sharpen your mind while familiarizing yourself with scripture, biblical events, and key figures from the bible.

BIBLE TRIVIA

Test your biblical knowledge with trivia questions covering scripture, biblical history, characters, and more. Perfect for players who love to learn through fun, competitive quizzes.

WORD SEARCH

Relax and reflect while searching for hidden words directly inspired by scripture, biblical stories, and essential themes. Ideal for quiet moments of meditation and learning.

Empowering the Knowledge of God

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone, through gaming, continuously deepens their understanding and experience of God's Kingdom—growing in His love, wisdom, and power. Every game we make is designed to leave players knowing God more intimately.

Who We Are

KingdomGames exists to reshape how people encounter, understand, and embrace the Kingdom of God. We believe gaming is a powerful and untapped medium for introducing millions to God's love, wisdom, and power.

Our Mission

"To bring the knowledge of God to all people by introducing, reshaping, and redefining everything about God and His Kingdom through gaming."
